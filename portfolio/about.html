About Me

Hello! I'm Chijike Samuel Ngozi Onyedianchi, a creative and detail-oriented web developer and programming student. I have a background in Sociology and Anthropology from Nnamdi Azikiwe University and am currently studying Web Development and Computer Programming at BYU-Idaho Pathway.

My journey into tech began with a passion for problem-solving and design. Over time, I've developed a growing interest in front-end web development, Python programming, and AI-powered solutions. I enjoy creating websites and tools that are clean, functional, and user-friendly projects that make people's lives easier or more enjoyable.

I'm currently working on projects such as a Study Assistant Bot (a smart personal study helper) and a Portfolio Website designed from scratch to build my professional presence online. My long-term goal is to combine my analytical and creative strengths to become a full-stack web developer and tech entrepreneur.

When I'm not coding, I love exploring new tech tools, playing football, and learning about how people interact with technology. I believe in continuous growth, collaboration, and building meaningful digital experiences one line of code at a time.
