Chijike Dev Portfolio - Site Plan

Author: Chijike Samuel Ngozi Onyedianchi

Site Name

Chijike Dev Portfolio

I specifically chose this name because it clearly represents my identity as a developer and establishes a professional brand. I view it as a simple, memorable, and suitable for use as a domain name.

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a personal portfolio to showcase my web development and programming projects, highlight my skills, and provide an easy way for potential clients, collaborators, or employers to contact me.

Scenarios

  • What projects has Chijike completed that demonstrates his skills?
  • How can I get in touch with Chijike to discuss a project or opportunity?

Color Schema

The site will use a professional blue-based palette with an accent color for highlights.

  • Primary Color: Navy Blue (#1E3A8A) - used for headers, navigation bar, and footer.
  • Accent Color: Bright Sky Blue (#38BDF8) - used for buttons, links, and highlights.
  • Neutral: White (#FFFFFF) and Light Gray (#F3F4F6) - used for background and body text.

Typography

The site will use two fonts:

  • Headings: Poppins, sans-serif
  • Body: Open Sans, sans-serif
